Meet your Instructor...

Jon MacLennan

My name is Jon Maclennan and I'm a full time guitarist. I've taught thousands of guitar lessons and am still looking forward to my next one. I've worked as a session player in New York and LA, toured a bit, and recorded for 3 different record labels.


I love to play in all genres --- for example I'm classically trained but I play in a rock band. I'm happy to teach guitar to anyone who has one, regardless of age, ability or style. Styles taught include: (everything), rock, reggae, country, blues, bluegrass, classical, avant-garde, Jazz, improv, metal, hip-hop, world- music, singer/songwriter.
