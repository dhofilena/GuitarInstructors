Critical Factor #1:

Passion

Passion... excitement... inspiration... They are the most important qualities for any great guitar teacher.

When someone loves what they’re doing, it comes through in the guitar lessons... the details... the inspiration... and the results.

Critical Factor #2:

Experience

Learning an instrument is a lifelong journey. Lessons are great because you get to benefit from someone who’s “Been there, done that”. You want to take advantage of their wisdom and experience.

A great guitar teacher is a perpetual student who’s always learning something new and exciting so he can share with his students.

A powerful sequential system

Too many guitar teachers teach whatever they “feel like” that day and throw together a mish-mash of random guitar lessons.

The worst part is -- it leaves the student feeling confused and frustrated.

And there’s nothing more sad than seeing a student quit because they had a bad teacher. Every great guitar teacher uses a sequential, step-by-step guitar system designed to maximize your progress.

Accelerated Learning Technology
